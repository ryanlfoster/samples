Conditions of Use

Open Use

Open use assets have licenses that permit open use for church publications and presentations. IP codes 0, 4, 7, and 9 fit within this category. The Codes are defined as follows.

0 Owned by Intellectual Reserve Incorporated (IRI), All uses permitted
4 Licensed for use by IRI entities only
7 Public domain. All uses permitted
9 Licensed for use by IRI and 3rd parties

Requires Approval

Assets in the requires approval groups have limitations on how the item may be used. In some cases permissions must be granted by a specific department or there may be conditional limitations the use of the product. Please work with your IP coordinator to get permission to use these items. IP Codes 0E, 0MR, 1, 1G, 4MR, {{! 1AV, 4AV}} 8 and 10 fall within this category. The codes are defined as follows.

{{! MISSING 1AV}} {{! MISSING 4AV}}
0E Department approval required to use the asset. IRI owned.
0MR IRI claims ownership. All uses permitted based on IPRA.
1 Not owner by IRI. Review the license agreement to determine usage.
1G Grandfathered product. Can only be used in its entirety.
4MR IRI claims licensing rights. Only for IRI use based on IPRA.
8 Not IRI owned, review the licensing agreement to determine usage rights.
10 Mixed elements. Each individual element is reported separately on the IP Report.

Restricted

Items with IP code 5 have unknown licensing rights. These items are not allowed for use until an IRI recognized contract is attached to the asset. Many items are added to MediaFinder without knowing the use conditions. These items are tagged as restricted until the status is updated.
